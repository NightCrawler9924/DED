Reflection

My Reflection

The Sustainability Summit was a great experience. I learned a lot about how to help the environment through better engineering and technology. It was inspiring to hear people talk about the importance of sustainability and how we can all make a difference. One of the most important things I learned is that sustainability should be a part of our everyday lives. I will try to use these ideas in my future work. I’m excited to share everything I learned with others at UBCO and encourage them to get involved in sustainability.

What UBCO Could Adopt from Other Schools

  • Collaborative Spaces for Innovation: Other schools offer more dedicated, flexible collaboration spaces for students during conferences and events, designed for brainstorming, networking, and hands-on workshops.
  • Live Event Streaming/Apps for Real-Time Updates: Some conferences use event apps or live-streaming platforms to share schedules, real-time updates, and speaker information, keeping participants informed.
  • Sustainability Initiatives: Many schools incorporate zero-waste policies and environmentally friendly practices, such as digital brochures, reusable materials, and eco-conscious catering.
  • International Collaboration: Some schools have strong international partnerships and bring in speakers or hold virtual events with global experts, creating opportunities for international collaboration.
