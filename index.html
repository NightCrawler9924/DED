About Me

Hello! I'm Deepansh Sabharwal, an engineering student at the University of British Columbia. I recently attended the CSE|CFES Conference, where I explored sustainability in engineering and connected with like-minded individuals passionate about creating a greener future.

What is CSE|CFES?

The Conference for Sustainability in Engineering (CSE) is an annual event organized by the Canadian Federation of Engineering Students (CFES). It brings together students from across Canada to discuss sustainable practices, innovative solutions, and the role of engineers in addressing global environmental challenges.
