My Experience

Reflecting on the event, here are a few suggestions for future improvement:

  • 🎤 More Interactive Workshops: Incorporating more hands-on sessions to enhance practical learning.
  • 🍽️ Better Meal Options: Expanding meal choices to accommodate various dietary restrictions and preferences.
  • 🚀 More Student-Led Panels: Creating opportunities for students to present their sustainability projects and ideas.
  • 📢 Enhanced Communication: Real-time event updates through an app or live feed for better coordination.
  • 🌍 Sustainability Initiatives: Promoting eco-friendly practices such as reusable materials, digital brochures, and zero-waste policies.
  • 💡 Collaboration Spaces: Providing breakout rooms for brainstorming and teamwork during events.
  • 🏅 Awards and Recognition: Highlighting student innovations through awards to encourage creativity and participation.
